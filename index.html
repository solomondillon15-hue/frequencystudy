📋 Study Overview

You will hear ten audio stimuli — five pure sine tones and five matched consonant triads across frequencies from 79 Hz to 639 Hz. After each 30-second stimulus, rate your emotional response.

  1. Wear headphones if possible. Use a quiet environment.
  2. Each stimulus lasts 30 seconds. Listen without interacting.
  3. After each tone, rate your valence and arousal (1–5 scale).
  4. Stimuli play in randomised order. You cannot skip once started.
  5. Responses save automatically and are downloadable as CSV.
